Heinzmann von Silenen

Version dated: 2011.11.25
Author/Author: Bernard Truffer Translation: Sergio Mantovani

approx. 1380/90 , mentioned the last time on 12.6.1450 , by Visp. Donzello. Descended from a family. noble urana, of which a branch in the early fourteenth century, following the marriage of Konrad, son of Arnold (->), with Aymonetta de Platea, had settled in Visp. The precise family ties are not known. He was castellan of Visp (1423, 1431 and 1443) and grand bailiff of Valais (1426-28, 1434 and 1439-41). In the affair of Raron (1415-20) he sided with the decanies against Witschard von Raron and in 1417 he was sent by the decany of Visp for the conclusion of the comboburghesia pact with the three forest countries.
